Welcome! You’ve just deployed a high-performance, dark-mode-first portfolio. This post is a quick guide on how to make it your own.

### 1. Update your Identity
Open `_config.yml` in your root directory. This is the "Brain" of the site. Change the following values:
* `title`: Your name or brand.
* `description`: What you do (e.g., Full Stack Developer).
* `email`: This is where your contact form will send messages.

### 2. Add Your Work
Head over to the `_data/` folder. This is where the magic happens without touching HTML:
* **Projects:** Edit `_data/projects.yml`.
* **Certificates:** Edit `_data/certificates.yml` to show off your verified badges.

### 3. Change the Theme Color
Want a different vibe? Open `assets/css/style.css` and change the `--primary-color` variable at the top. Everything from buttons to links will update automatically!

Happy Coding!
